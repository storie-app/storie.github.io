Substack to TikTok in Seconds

Turn written content into engaging kinetic typography videos in seconds.

Our app helps creators, writers, marketers, and brands instantly transform articles, newsletters, and text content into short-form videos designed for platforms like TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts.

How it works

1. Import your content

Connect your content source or paste text directly into the app. Users can import newsletters, articles, posts, or written content.

2. Generate video automatically

The app automatically converts text into animated kinetic-typography videos with transitions, timing, and layouts.

3. Publish your content

Export videos for TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, and other social platforms while maintaining your voice and brand.

Features

  • Convert newsletters and long-form text into short videos
  • Automatic kinetic typography generation
  • Fast video creation in seconds
  • Brand-focused content generation
  • Optimized for social media sharing
